Employment Options is a free service available to anyone impacted by the 2013 Boston Marathon bombing. We will assist you in considering your career choices and getting you back in the workforce. We can also help you retain your current job and address any workplace challenges.

Our services are customized to meet your needs. We provide consultations in person and via phone, email, and videoconference. Depending on your location and your needs, weâ€™ll work with you to nd the best format for our meetings.

Our services include:

  • Working one-on-one with a trained vocational specialist
  • Career guidance and support as you consider entering employment or to help you retain your current job
  • Career exploration activities to determine good job matches based on your skills, interests, and abilities
  • Advice and consultation on job search techniques, including networking, online applications, and personality tests
  • Access to other employment services, such as vocational rehabilitation
  • Help identifying and applying to jobs that t your career goals
  • Finding community resources to address other employment-related needs, such as transportation
  • Assistance with reasonable accommodations and/or assistive technology
